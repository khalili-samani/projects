# DFO South Wharf Fashion Outlet Sales Dashboard

## Business Context

This project simulates a sales analytics dashboard for a fashion outlet store located at DFO South Wharf in Melbourne.

The store operates in a discount-heavy retail environment where management needs to monitor revenue, gross margin, promotional performance, returns, product performance and staff sales activity.

## Business Questions

1. How is the store performing over time?
2. Which products and categories generate the most revenue and profit?
3. Are discounts improving sales or damaging margin?
4. Which staff members are driving the strongest sales outcomes?
5. What products or categories have unusually high return rates?
6. Are there data quality issues that could affect reporting accuracy?

## Target Users

- Store Manager
- Area Manager
- Retail Operations Manager
- Merchandise Planner

## Dashboard Pages

1. Executive Overview
2. Product & Category Performance
3. Discount & Promotion Analysis
4. Returns & Data Quality
5. Staff Performance

## Success Criteria

The dashboard should allow a non-technical retail manager to understand performance within 30 seconds and identify clear actions.
